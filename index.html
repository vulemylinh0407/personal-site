about me

Hello, my name is (Le My) Linh Vu, please call me Lynn. I’m a Vietnamese graphic designer based in Ontario, Canada. I am most interested in editorial and illustration. I also love working with typography and web design.

My strength is my ability to create designs that are not only aesthetic but also practical and functional. I value beautiful designs with beautiful messages and meanings. That’s why I always start my design process with researching and brainstorming.

One thing I have learned as a graphic designer is a project solely designed on the computer takes away its originality. By starting a project brainstorming and sketching, I have improved my design and illustration skills greatly. Thus, my designs have personalities and are more thoughtful.

Above all, I'm always eager to learn new things and improve my skills.
